Hi,
What a year, huh?

I don’t know what’s the past year been for you, and I’d love to read it you if you are willing to share. And if you are feeling up for it, have a read of this blogpost, it might resonate.
On my side, you can read a bit on the blog. And maybe it’s because I feel a little rough around the edges, but I feel like even this email is a little rushed, a little frayed. Well, even if so…

I wish you to find a way to not only fit into your skin, but to fill it to the brim.
To be yourself so fully, so truthfully, so unconditionally, that you never doubt who you really are.
I wish you to always be comfortable with the price you have to pay for being that specific version of yourself.

I wish you clarity; inexplicable and deep understanding of who you are and what you want.
And the knowledge when to proceed with kindness and softness, and when to wreck whatever you must to clear a path for your future.

I wish you bravery. For facing whatever comes next, as well as for the things you are so tirelessly running away from. For taking the leap, and making the jump. For being.

I wish you persistence. May the next year be the one in which you learn a new skill you so desperately want to, be it drawing, juggling or the ability to forgive yourself.

I wish you to achieve your goals. May the world yield to your will, and not the other way around.
May you never be at the wrong end of unknown gods’ whims. May you find your claws and know how to use them.

I wish you strength. And to not settle for less than what you deserve. Whether it’s an extra piece of French toast, salary increase or the fall of the government.

I wish you good people. And likeminded ones. May the right people find you at the right time. May it be reciprocal.

I wish for peace. I wish you the same.

And above all, I wish you gentleness. Give yourself the same care that you’d give to those closest to you.

‎ 

‎ 


With wishes for more kindness,

Adi / Zmeyche

Panagyurishte, 25/12/2025
